Waste Sorting - Recycle/Organic options and ML model

At our waste management website, we understand that sorting waste into recyclable and organic categories can be confusing for many citizens. To make this process simpler and more efficient, we have implemented a machine learning model that can automatically classify waste into its respective category.

When a citizen uploads a picture of their waste item, our ML model analyzes it and determines whether it is recyclable or organic. The classification is then displayed on the user's profile, making it easier for them to dispose of their waste responsibly. Similarly, when a garbage collector scans the QR code on the citizen's profile, they can view the classification of the waste, which helps them sort it into the correct category.

The ML model has several benefits for both citizens and garbage collectors. It eliminates the need for citizens to research and determine whether an item is recyclable or organic, saving them time and effort. For garbage collectors, it streamlines the waste sorting process, making it more efficient and accurate.
